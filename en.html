Sessions of Reconnective Healing

Reconnective Healing is a healing method developed by chiropractor Eric Pearl. This technique focuses on the idea that there is a network of energies and frequencies that can facilitate healing on physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual levels. At the body level, there has been a significant increase in thousands of cases where individuals have experienced complete healing or improvement in serious conditions such as rheumatoid arthritis, cancer, cerebral palsy, fibromyalgia, etc.


Benefits

Energies and Frequencies: Reconnective Healing involves working with specific energies and frequencies that have the capacity to influence the energetic system of the human body. These frequencies are connected to a broader network of universal energies.
On an emotional level: These sessions contribute to the healing and release of traumas, tensions, panic attacks, depression, anxiety, insomnia, and stress.
Physical Healing: Practitioners claim that Reconnective Healing can contribute to the healing or improvement of physical conditions, including serious illnesses such as cancer and fibromyalgia.
Reconnection with the Universal Network: Eric Pearl asserts that Reconnective Healing is not only about treating specific conditions but also about reconnecting the individual with the universal network of energies, thereby contributing to overall balance and healing.
Restoration of Spiritual Integrity: In addition to the physical aspect, Reconnective Healing also claims to support the restoration of spiritual integrity by releasing blockages or interferences that may keep a person separated from the harmony and perfection of the universe.
For Women: Reconnective Healing can address issues such as menstrual pain, endometriosis, ovarian cysts, reproductive system dysfunctions, hormonal imbalances, and post-traumatic stress.
For Men: This method may contribute to treating reproductive system dysfunctions and prostate conditions.
For Children: Significant healings at a physical level and improvements in relationships with parents and close ones have been recorded.
Assistance in Addiction Treatment: Helps individuals quit smoking, alcohol consumption, and drug use, providing an alternative approach to overcoming these issues.


Healing Sessions

About the session: During a Reconnective Healing session, the practitioner establishes a connection with these energies and frequencies, facilitating their interaction with the client's energetic system. This is done through hand movements, without physical touch
Number of sessions: Between 1 and 3 sessions
Session length 30 minutes is the duration of each session
Procedure: Conducted remotely via phone call.
